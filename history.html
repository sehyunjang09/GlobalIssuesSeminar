A Brief History of
Deforestation

The Past, Present and the Future of Earth

Classical Period

3000 BCE ~ 500 CE

Deforestation is a matter of the destruction of forests due to various causes. It could be caused by deliberate fires, or plainly chopping down trees. Forests have been, and still are being, destroyed all around the world.
