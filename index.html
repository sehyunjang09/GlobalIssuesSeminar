What is Deforestation?

Deforestation is a matter on the destruction of forests due to various causes. It could be caused by deliberate fires, or plainly chopping down trees. Forests have been, and still are being destroyed all around the world.

Still a long way to go

Wood has been one of the most versatile materials. Deforestation continued for a long time to fuel our society. It also happened to acquire more land for various uses. People have, and still are, burning large portions of forests for it.

The Earth needs your help

Deforestation causes our home planet to slowly decay. To save it, we should all take part.
